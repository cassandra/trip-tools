Locations

All the places.

The Problems

  • There is no centralized place to accumulate information about the places in your trip.
    • Sources and links: e.g., who recommeneded it and what did they have to say.
    • It's location on a map.
    • How interesting is it to you? e.g., must-see, marginal
    • What's its rating and from where?
    • Pricing? Days and hours they are open
    • Contact information and links.
    • Does it need advanced booking? Have you booked it yet?
    • Have you written a review about it

The Solution

  • Location is the central concept to tie all trip data together.
  • The physical location is a primary attribute for trip planning and execution.
    • It is required for transportation planning.
    • It is essential for deciding on lodging.
    • It is important for planning daily itineraries.
  • Visual plot of locations on a map to use in planning a trip, while on the trip and when journaling about the trip.

Existing Tools

  • Google Maps
    • Great for visually plotting locations.
    • Bookmarks do not allow richer set of attributes.
    • The "My Maps" feature is better, but treated like a step-child with a clunky interface.
    • "My Maps" has some, but limited means of adding more attributes.
    • Does not integrate well with anything else.
