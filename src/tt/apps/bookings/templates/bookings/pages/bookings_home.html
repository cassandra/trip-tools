Bookings

Manage your bookings for the trip.

The Problems

  • The things you have already booked are scattered across many sites.
  • There's no good connection between things you need to book versus things you already booked.
  • The things you need to book requires you to keep a to-do list and/or create calendar reminders.
  • What you've booked and your itinerary are tightly related, but not identical.
  • Tracking shared expenses is complicated.

The Solution

  • A tight integration with your itinerary with bookings really just being the financial view.
  • Allow creation of "to-do" items to be booked with optional timeframes and reminders.
  • Seemless connection between your itinerary and bookings.
  • Tracking cancellation policies.
  • Tracking payment methods and dates charged or to-be charged.
  • Ability to track shared expenses.

Existing Tools

  • TripIt
    • Great for aggregating existing bookings, but not to-do bookings.
    • Good for building itinerary, but not everything in an itinerary maps to a booking.
    • The user interface is relatively poor.
    • Seems to be lightly supported since they were bought.
    • Used to have an API which would be great to itegrate with, but is no longer offered .
  • Tricount
    • Great tool for tracking shared expenses, but not integrated with anything else.
