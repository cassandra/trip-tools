Planning

Plan and compare options for your trip.

The Problems

  • Research and comparison of options is scattered across browser tabs, notes, and spreadsheets.
  • No central place to track candidates for lodging, transportation, activities, etc.
  • Hard to compare options side-by-side with custom attributes that matter to you.
  • Decisions made during planning don't flow into bookings and itinerary.
  • When planning with others, everyone has their own separate research.

The Solution

  • A central place to collect and organize all your trip options.
  • Group candidates by type (lodging, flights, activities, restaurants, etc.).
  • Add custom attributes for comparison (price, distance, amenities, ratings).
  • Rank and prioritize options with preference ordering.
  • Seamless flow from planning decisions to bookings and itinerary.
  • Shared planning workspace for trip collaborators.

Existing Tools

  • Spreadsheets
    • Flexible for custom comparisons, but disconnected from other trip data.
    • Hard to share and collaborate on effectively.
    • No integration with booking or itinerary workflows.
  • Browser Bookmarks
    • Quick to save links, but no structure or comparison capability.
    • Easy to lose track of what you've already researched.
  • Note-taking Apps
    • Good for freeform notes, but lack structured comparison features.
    • Not purpose-built for trip planning workflows.
