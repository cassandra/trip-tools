Reviews

Manage reviews related to this trip.

The Problems

  • Reviews for different categories of things need to be posted to different places.
  • The same review might need to be posted to multiple places.
  • You want to have a place to put your "raw" review thoughts for later word-smithing
  • You want to refer to, or copy parts of your reviews into a travel log.

The Solution

  • Provide a central place to capture all your reviews, both in their draft and refined forms.
  • Integration with the Trip Tools Journal feature will allow easy reference while authoring your journal entries without having to remember where and what you posted.
  • Initially, you can then cut-and-paste to the sites you want, with a deeper integration to help post as a longer term feature.

Existing Tools

  • Booking.com
    • Good for hotel reviews since they require confirmed stays.
    • Broadening business scope has them losing focus on lodging: not a good sign for the future.
  • Tripadvisor
    • Maybe still OK for tour and activity-related reviews?
    • Has already lost focus on the core travel features: money, money, money.
  • Google
    • Good for restaurant reviews since most convenient when looking things up on a map.
  • A Million Other Sites
    • Too many sites. Too much spam. Too hard to find a trustworthy source.
