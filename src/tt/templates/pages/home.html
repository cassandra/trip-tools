Trip Tools

Open source trip planning, management, and journaling

There are lots of great tools for managing trips. The problem? You need to use lots of them. Planning in one app, bookings in another, itineraries in a spreadsheet, photos scattered everywhere. We believe the entire lifecycle of a trip is one problem - and we're building the solution.

Why Trip Tools?

A trip isn't just planning, or just managing bookings, or just sharing photos afterward. It's all of these things, connected. But existing tools force you to juggle multiple apps, cut and paste between them, maintain separate logins, and somehow keep everything in sync.

Trip Tools takes a different approach: one place for your entire trip lifecycle. Pre-trip research and planning. Comparing options and making decisions. Managing bookings and itineraries. Capturing memories during the trip. Writing and sharing your travel journal afterward. All connected, all in one place.

We're building this because we needed it ourselves. And we're building it open source because we believe travel tools should work for travelers, not for ad revenue.

Available Now: Journal & Travelog

We're starting with the post-trip experience - because that's where your memories live. The Journal feature lets you write rich travel journals with photos, then publish them as beautiful public travelogs to share with friends and family.

  • Rich text editor with drag-and-drop image insertion
  • Auto-save with conflict detection - never lose your work
  • Flexible publishing - private, password-protected, or public
  • 6 color themes for your published travelog
  • Version history - publish updates while preserving previous versions

Where We're Headed

The Journal is just the beginning. We're building toward a complete trip management platform that handles every phase of your journey:

Coming Soon

  • Planning - Compare options for lodging, transportation, and activities
  • Locations - Organize all your places with maps and rich details
  • Itineraries - Day-by-day schedules connected to your locations and bookings
  • Bookings - Track what's booked, what's pending, and shared expenses
  • Reviews - Capture your thoughts on places you visit

Early Access & Contributing

Trip Tools is in early development. We're currently invite-only while we build out the core features and ensure a stable experience for early users.

This is an open source project, and we welcome contributors of all kinds - whether you want to write code, improve the design, or just share feedback on what would make this useful for your trips.
